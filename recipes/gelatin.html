Gelatin recipe

Here is a quick and easy recipe for gelatin. The estimated cooking time is less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • gelatin mix
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup cold water
  1. Pour gelatin mix into a bowl and mix with 1 cup hot water until dissolved.
  2. Slowly pour in 1 cup cold water and mix thoroughly.
  3. Set completed mixture in fridge until gelatin sets.
