Hard Boiled Eggs recipe

Here is a quick and easy recipe for hard boiled eggs. The estimated cooking time is less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • water
  1. Add eggs to a saucepan and fill with water until eggs are submerged with 1 inch of water
  2. Boil to your desired yolk firmness.
  3. Boil for 4 minutes for softer yolks, or up to 16 minutes for firmer yolks.
