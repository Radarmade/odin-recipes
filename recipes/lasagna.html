Lasagna

Quick and easy Lasagna recipe.

Here is a simple recipe for Lasagna. It is made using lasagna noodles, cheese, meat, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown meat.
  3. Add cooked ingredients to baking dish.
  4. Add cheese and tomato sauce.
  5. Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes.
